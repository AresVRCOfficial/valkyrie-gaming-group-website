About Us: Valkyrie Gaming Group

Valkyrie Gaming Group, formerly known as TMB Gaming Group, is a diverse community of gamers united by our love for gaming, creativity, and friendship. Whether you're a competitive player seeking glory in esports or a casual gamer looking for fun adventures, there's a place for you among the Valkyries.

Our Mission

  • Inclusivity: We welcome gamers of all backgrounds, skill levels, and interests, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where everyone can thrive and have fun.
  • Community Bonding: Forge lasting friendships, share memorable gaming moments, and explore new virtual worlds together as part of our tight-knit community.
  • Diverse Gaming Experiences: From intense esports competitions to laid-back adventures in games like Minecraft, VRChat, Garry's Mod, and more, there's something for everyone at Valkyrie Gaming Group.

What We Offer

  • Valkyrie Vanguard Esports Team: Join our competitive teams in games like Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO), Overwatch 2, League of Legends, Fortnite, and more as we aim for victory and sportsmanship on the global stage.
  • Casual Gaming Community: Dive into casual games such as Minecraft, VRChat, Garry's Mod (GMod), Among Us, and various multiplayer titles with fellow Valkyries who share your passion for fun and exploration.
  • Creative Projects: Collaborate on creative projects, build amazing worlds, host events, and showcase your talents through content creation, roleplaying, and community-driven initiatives.
  • Regular Events: Participate in community game nights, themed events, tournaments, workshops, and social gatherings that cater to a wide range of gaming interests and preferences.

Join Valkyrie Gaming Group

Whether you're a hardcore gamer, a casual player, a content creator, or someone looking to make new friends in the gaming world, Valkyrie Gaming Group offers a welcoming and vibrant community where you can be yourself and share unforgettable gaming experiences.
